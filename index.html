  • Thousands of Japanese Vending machines are programmed to
    provide free refreshments and food during times of emergency
    like a tsunami or earthquake. They contain backup generators in
    order to be able to do this.
  • Japan has approximately 1 vending machine for every 23 people.
  • Vending machine sales total more than 60 million USD anually.
  • There are so many vending machines that there are no more
    available slots to own one.
  • There are vending machines that carry specialty items, for
    example, there are vending machines that carry items such as
    bananas, burgers, rice, eggs, and apples.
